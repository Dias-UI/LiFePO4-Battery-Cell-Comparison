Battery Cell Comparison

REPT (GSL) | Cornex | Dejin (Easyway) - Technical Analysis & Procurement Guide

Executive Summary: This comparison analyzes seven leading Grade A LiFePO4 battery cell manufacturers - REPT (GSL), Cornex, Dejin (Easyway), CATL, CALB, EVE, and A123 Systems - evaluating their technical specifications, performance characteristics, and ideal applications. All manufacturers offer traceability, long cycle life (3000-8000+ cycles), and reliable safety features, with differences in capacity range, energy density, pricing positioning, and target markets from automotive to industrial energy storage.

REPT (Premium)

Excel in safety certifications and automotive-grade durability. Ideal for high-end, critical applications but at higher cost. Best for mission-critical industrial deployments.

Cornex (Balanced)

Deliver excellent balance for large-scale energy storage with superior cycle life, energy density, and safety. Perfect for scalable commercial solar and EV systems.

Dejin (Cost-Effective)

Offer reliable, safe, and cost-effective solutions. Suitable for DIY, off-grid solar, and smaller energy storage projects with solid quality backing.

CATL (Industry Leader)

Global benchmark for LiFePO4 performance and safety. Powers major EV manufacturers like Tesla, NIO, XPeng. Premium pricing with proven reliability.

CALB (Commercial Focus)

Strong presence in electric buses and industrial systems. Excellent for large-scale commercial applications with robust cycle life.

EVE (Versatile)

Competitive mid-range option for diverse applications. Good balance of performance and cost for EVs and energy storage.

A123 (High Power)

Specialized in high-power applications. Ideal for motorsports, automotive, and UPS systems requiring rapid discharge.
Decision Matrix: For mission-critical industrial applications requiring automotive-grade durability, choose REPT or CATL. For large-scale commercial energy storage projects prioritizing energy density and cycle life, select Cornex or CALB. For cost-sensitive DIY or off-grid solar installations, opt for Dejin or EVE. For high-power automotive and motorsports applications, A123 Systems excels with rapid discharge capabilities. Note: CATL is widely recognized as the industry benchmark for LiFePO4 performance and safety, powering major EV manufacturers globally including Tesla, NIO, and XPeng, though at premium pricing.
